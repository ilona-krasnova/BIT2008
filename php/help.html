FAQ

What is Seed + Share?

Seed + Share is a website where you can browse and shop for different kinds of plant seeds.

So how does it work?

You can browse our catalogue or use the search feature to find seeds you like. Once you find a seed you like, send a request to the user who posted the seed. You must pick a seed you have listed to swap with the seed you are requesting. If they accept your request, you will exchange your seed for their seed (swap), and contact info will be shared and a chat feature will become available to finalize the swap.

I am not from Canada. Can I still participate?

Sorry, but no! Due to postal laws, we are not able to ship outside of Canada.

Seed information

What makes a good Seed + Share listing?

  • A concise and descriptive name, e.g. French Marigold
  • A clear, well-lit photo of your plant
  • A description that tells other users information such as:
    • Soil requirements, e.g. "Needs good drainage"
    • Resistance to certain animals
    • When to plant
    • Whether to plant indoors or outdoors
    • Flowers: bloom time
    • Vegetables: harvest times

What is a plant's lifecycle?

There are three lifecycles, annual, biennual and perennial. Annual plants only last one season, they will die after that. Biennual plants will live for 2 years, then they will die. Perennials come back every year, and will live for many years.

What are sun requirements?

Every type of plant requires a different amount of sun exposure. Some need to be planted in full sun, part sun, part shade or full shade.

What is a hardiness zone?

Hardiness zones are the climates where certain plants are able to grow. Please keep this in mind when picking plant seeds.
